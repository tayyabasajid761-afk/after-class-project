FACTS ABOUT CAT

Cats are fascinating creatures with unique biology, possessing more bones than humans, a specialized organ for "tasting" scents (Jacobson's organ), and whiskers that act as sensory GPS, helping them navigate and fit into spaces.

Sensory Skills: Their rough tongues can clean bones, their whiskers help them navigate, and they possess superior night vision and hearing, making them efficient predators.
